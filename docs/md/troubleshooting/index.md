****** Troubleshooting Guide ​ ******
This guide will help you quickly find the right troubleshooting article based
on what you're seeing or experiencing with your Electra One controller. Just
match the symptoms with the examples below to get started.
***** The controler fails to start ​ *****
    * Stuck on startup animation or splash screen?
      → Stuck_at_Startup
      This may be caused by a problematic preset or Lua script—often when
      you're developing a new preset or if a preset becomes corrupted on the SD
      card.
    * Controller shows a black screen and doesn’t boot at all?
      → Bricked_After_Update
      Firmware update may have failed or installed an invalid image.
    * Brief backlight flash but no startup animation?
      → Dead_SD_Card
      SD card may be corrupted, unreadable, or completely dead.
***** Power-Related Instability ​ *****
    * Controller keeps restarting after boot?
    * Screen turns completely white?
    * Random ghost touches or erratic display behavior?
      → Power-Related_Issues
      It may be caused by a weak USB power supply or bad cable.
***** Display Problems ​ *****
    * Fonts look broken, unreadable, or misaligned?
      → Broken_Fonts
      The UI toolkit asset file may be missing or corrupted on SD card.
***** Device Not Found ​ *****
    * Electra One not detected by the web app?
    * CTRL MIDI ports not visible on macOS or Windows?
      → Controller_Not_Detected
      It may be USB connection, driver, or MIDI configuration issues.
***** Problems with USB Disk ​ *****
    * USB Disk mode not working
    * File explorer crashing
      → USB_Disk_mode_issues
      It may be caused by incompatibility of Electra's USB disk driver and your
      operating system.
***** Nothing helped? ​ *****
If you’re not sure what’s wrong or the issue isn’t covered here, feel free to:
    * Visit the Electra_One_Community_Forum
    * Contact Electra One support via email for direct assistance
